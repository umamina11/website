About The Game

The object of the game is to solve the equation displayed on the screen and then choose the correct option from the option list given at the right hand side of the screen.If the answer selected is right another equation would appear on the screen otherwise a beep sound is emmitted to show that the answer is wrong.You can also choose from one the operations given at the top right of the screen.If you are accurate enough you will advance further in the game
