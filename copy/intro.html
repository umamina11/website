INSTRUCTIONS

The object of the game is to rearrange the letters to form a word.

  • Study the scrambled letters and try to unscramble or rearrange the letters to form a word.

  • Arrange the letters in the correct sequence to form a word.

  • Click the "Guess" button to see if your guess is correct.

  • If your answer was correct, then click the "Next Word" button.

  • If your answer was wrong, then try again.
